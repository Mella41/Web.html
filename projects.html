Trapped Ion

For the HardHaQ ’25 IonQ challenge, my team and I designed and optimized a 3D RF Paul trap using COMSOL Multiphysics. We adjusted key geometric parameters, including rod spacing, rod length, endcap placement, and RF voltage to study how each change affected the electric potential, RF field distribution, and overall trapping behavior of a Yb⁺ ion. We evaluated performance using IonQ’s Trap Metrics, focusing on improving trap depth, reducing offset, and balancing power efficiency with confinement stability. Through iterative simulation, visualization, and analysis, we produced an optimized trap design along with detailed metrics and geometry renderings that demonstrated clear improvements and strong understanding of trapped-ion hardware fundamentals.
